3. Other Extension Monitoring

In version 3.2.0, the management permission has been completely removed from the manifest.json file.

The extension no longer requests this permission, meaning it has zero ability to see what other extensions you have installed on your browser.

Verdict: NOT tracked. (Permission Removed)

5. Mouse Tracking

The extension ignores your general mouse movements, clicks, and scrolling speed. The only mouse-tracking code inside the extension is activated exclusively when you use the "Report a Bug" feature to draw a screenshot rectangle.

Verdict: NOT tracked.
